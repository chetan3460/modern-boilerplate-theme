Overview\n\n### Alpine.js Component\n```javascript\nx-data=\"accordion({\n    allowMultiple: true/false,\n    animated: true/false\n})\"\n```\n\n**State Management**\n- `openItems[]` - Array of open item indices\n- `toggleItem(index)` - Toggle item open/closed\n- `closeAll()` - Close all items\n- `openAll()` - Open all items\n\n### ACF Fields\n```\nAccordion Block\n├── Hide Block (true_false)\n├── Title (text)\n├── Subtitle (textarea)\n├── Accordion Items (repeater)\n│   ├── Item Title (text)\n│   └── Item Content (wysiwyg)\n└── Accordion Settings (group)\n    ├── Allow Multiple Open (true_false)\n    ├── Animated (true_false)\n    └── Background Color (select)\n```\n\n### Design Options\n- **Animations**: Smooth or instant\n- **Behavior**: Single or multiple items open\n- **Colors**: Light Blue, White, or Grey background\n- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly\n- **Accessibility**: ARIA compliant\n\n## Quick Reference\n\n### Setup Timeline\n1. **Admin**: Add
